Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Bacon 200g
Eggs 4
Parmesan 50g
Black Pepper To taste
Step 1: Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente.
Step 2: Fry the bacon in a pan until crispy.
Step 3: Whisk the eggs with Parmesan and black pepper.
Step 4: Combine the cooked spaghetti with bacon and pour the egg mixture over. Toss until creamy.

Source: BBC Good Food

Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breast 2 pieces
Lettuce 1 head
Tomatoes 2
Cucumber 1
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Step 1: Grill the chicken breasts until fully cooked.
Step 2: Slice the chicken and vegetables.
Step 3: Toss the salad with olive oil and seasonings.

Source: Food Network

Vegetable Stir Fry

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Mixed Vegetables (Carrot, Bell Peppers, Broccoli) 3 cups
Soy Sauce 3 tbsp
Tofu 200g
Rice 2 cups
Step 1: Cook the rice according to package instructions.
Step 2: Stir-fry the vegetables and tofu in soy sauce until tender.
Step 3: Serve the stir fry on top of the rice.

Source: AllRecipes
